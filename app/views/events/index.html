Potluck Details

If you’re planning on bringing a dish -- thank you! We can’t wait to see what you cook up! We’re still figuring out logistics for dropping off your dish check back fro more info soon! Want to see what other people are bringing?

Portion Size

There will be around 150 people at our wedding, and we absolutely do not expect you to bring enough food to feed everyone. Instead, please consider bringing the portion that you would make for a large dinner party. Between all of us, we’ll have more than enough food to share.

Reheating

We’ll have staff on site to help re-heat any dishes that need it. But, we’ll have limited oven space, so we’d like to plan ahead. If your dish needs to be reheated before serving, let us know on your RSVP. If you bring your dish hot, we’ll have chafers ready to keep it warm until dinner time. Please bring your dish fully cooked -- we can’t do any actual cooking at the venue, but we can re-heat it!

Bringing your Dish

Please bring your food in a disposable tray or bowl. We’re trying to minimize cleanup time. If you’re bringing something that won’t work in a disposable dish, send Anna an email and we can figure it out! annasalomone@gmail.com. Something like this works great.

Serving

We’re renting chafers for dishes that need to be warm, and everything else will be served in the dishes they come in. No need to bring serving utensils; we’ve got you covered.

Food Sensitivities

We’ll have both vegetarian and meat main dishes. If you have any food sensitivities/restrictions (vegan, gluten free, etc.), we suggest incorporating those into the dish you make so we can make sure there’s something for everyone to eat.

Labeling Your Dish

We’ll make up formal food labels (based on the dish you RSVP with) so people know what they’re eating. If you could label your dish in some way (post it note, piece of paper, sharpie on the tinfoil covering it -- we’re not picky), so we can match dishes to the printed food labels, we would be grateful. Also, if your dish is vegetarian, vegan, or gluten free, we’d appreciate you noting that as well.
